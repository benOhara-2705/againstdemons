ABOUT THIS SITE

Hi! Welcome to Against Demons, an UNOFFICIAL site dedicated to showcasing all music related to the British band Radiohead.

This site comes from an idea to create a display of the bands discography in a way that new users (or even long-time fans like myself) can easily navigate it.

The reason being that, on the band's official Radiohead Public Library (which was a big inspiration for this site, so thank you!), there is quite a lot of extra content present that, while cool for hardcore fans, can be quite redundant for people who just want to hear their music.

As the band's discography can be quite hard to navigate without all this extra content, I thought it would be easier to have a place on the internet where users can simply access releases from them without having to go through stuff they don't need to see.

Nothing too personal, this site is just a simple dedication to the band that made me realise what music could be.

If you encounter any errors, bugs or crashes, feel free to let me know at: ben259a123@gmail.com

Thanks for visiting! I hope you find what you're looking for.

Ben
